About these materials

The two literacy courses on this site (Witsuwit'en Literacy 1 and Witsuwit'en Literacy 2) were taught by Sharon Hargus over two terms in 2019 and 2020 for the Witsuwit'en language program. The worksheets, answer keys, and video recordings on this site are the materials from those classes, posted here as a self-paced resource.

Author

Sharon Hargus, Professor of Linguistics, University of Washington.

Hosting

The HTML, CSS, and PDFs on this site are hosted on GitHub Pages. The video recordings are hosted on the Internet Archive and stream directly into each session's page.

Permissions and reuse

The materials are shared for educational use. If you'd like to adapt or redistribute them, please get in touch first.

Sister sites

This site is a sibling of Witsuwit'en Texts, Sahaptin Texts, Kwa'wala Texts, and the Deg Xinag collections.
