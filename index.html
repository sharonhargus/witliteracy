Witsuwit'en Literacy

Self-paced lessons in reading and writing Witsuwit'en

This site collects the materials I developed 5–6 years ago for the Witsuwit'en language program's literacy training. There are two volumes, each with eight self-contained sessions:

  • Witsuwit'en Literacy 1 introduces the Witsuwit'en writing system itself — consonants, vowels, diacritics, and a brief history of the orthography.
  • Witsuwit'en Literacy 2 works through grammar fundamentals — possessive prefixes, vowel contrasts, verb conjugation, and related patterns.

Each session has a worksheet, an answer key (volume 2 only), and a video recording of the live classroom session that walks through the material. Worksheets are PDF downloads served from this site. Videos are hosted on the Internet Archive and stream directly into each session's page — they may take a moment to load on a slow connection.

Work through them in order or jump to whatever's most useful.
